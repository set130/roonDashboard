# ✅ Year Dropdown Now Scrollable!

## Fixed
The year dropdown now shows **60 years** and is **scrollable** so you can pick any year like 2009!

## What Changed

### Year Range Expanded
- **Before:** Only 10 years (2021-2031)
- **After:** 60 years (1976-2036)

### Dropdown is Scrollable
- ✅ Max height: 200px
- ✅ Scrollbar appears automatically
- ✅ Scroll to find any year
- ✅ Can now select 2009, 1995, 2030, etc.

## How to Use

1. Click "Pick date"
2. Click the year dropdown
3. **Scroll down** in the dropdown to find older years
4. **Scroll up** for newer years
5. Click the year you want (e.g., 2009)

## Year Range (as of 2026)

- **Oldest:** 1976 (50 years back)
- **Newest:** 2036 (10 years forward)
- **Total:** 61 years available

**Refresh your browser**, open the date picker, and scroll through the year dropdown to select 2009 or any other year!
