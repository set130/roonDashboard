# ✅ Date Picker Enhanced!

## What's New

The year selector in the date picker is now a **dropdown** (like months) and you can **scroll** to change years!

## Features

### Year Dropdown
- ✅ Shows **10 years** (5 before, 5 after current year)
- ✅ Click to select from list
- ✅ Range updates automatically
- ✅ Same style as month dropdown

### Scroll to Navigate Years
- ✅ **Scroll up** → Earlier year
- ✅ **Scroll down** → Later year
- ✅ Works when calendar is open
- ✅ Fast year navigation

## How to Use

1. **Click "Pick date"** to open calendar
2. **Year dropdown:**
   - Click year dropdown
   - Select from 10 years shown
3. **Scroll:**
   - Hover over calendar
   - Scroll mouse wheel up/down
   - Year changes instantly
